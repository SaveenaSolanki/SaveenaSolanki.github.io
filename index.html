Research interests

My work sits at the interface of machine learning and molecular biology. I am interested in representation learning for chemistry and proteins, structure-aware deep models for signalling and drug discovery, and turning large biological datasets into mechanistic, testable hypotheses. I also care about clear scientific communication and illustration.

Deep learning for molecules

Multimodal representation learning that fuses structure, sequence and chemical descriptors into transferable embeddings.

GPCR & signalling

How endogenous sterols and small molecules allosterically modulate the GPCR–Gα interface.

Mechanism-aware ML

Models that respect chemistry — mapping redox landscapes and reaction mechanisms rather than fitting black boxes.

Systems biology

Biological networks as a lens on health and disease, bridging molecular data and modern medicine.
